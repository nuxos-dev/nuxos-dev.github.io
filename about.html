About NuxOS

A Purpose-Built Windows-Based
Operating System

NuxOS is a community-driven OS designed with one clear principle: an operating system should serve its purpose, not pretend to serve everyone.

Overview

Not a One-Size-Fits-All Solution

NuxOS is not an all-in-one solution. It is a family of editions, each engineered for a specific workload, audience, and long-term goal.

From servers and enterprise environments to workstations and gaming systems, NuxOS provides clearly defined choices — without unnecessary compromises.

Philosophy

Built by Purpose, Not by Trend

Most modified operating systems attempt to be everything at once: lightweight, gaming-ready, server-capable, secure, and beginner-friendly — often at the cost of clarity and reliability.

NuxOS takes a different path. Every edition has a clear target use case, a defined set of trade-offs, and a responsibility to do exactly what it claims. Nothing more. Nothing less.

You choose the edition. You accept its philosophy. We ensure it delivers.

Design Principles

What NuxOS Stands For

Clean by Design

Focused on removing noise, not functionality. Core Windows features remain intact unless there is a clear, technical reason to change them.

Stability First

Every stable release prioritizes predictability, long-term reliability, and controlled updates — especially for enterprise and server environments.

Transparency

No hidden scripts. No undocumented changes. No "magic tweaks" without explanation. Users should understand what their system does and why.

Minimal, Not Broken

Minimalism means fewer unnecessary processes, not missing capabilities. The goal is efficiency without sacrificing essential Windows behavior.

Editions

Each Built for a Specific Role

Server Edition
Designed for high-performance and long-running workloads. Minimal footprint, hardened configuration, and optimized background services for reliability and uptime.
Enterprise (LTS)
Focused on stability at scale. Long-term support releases with conservative updates, suitable for production systems, virtualization, and mission-critical environments.
Workstation
Tailored for developers, creators, and professionals. Balanced performance, full tooling support, and optimized resource management for daily productivity.
Gaming Edition
Performance-oriented configuration with reduced background overhead. Pre-tuned for low latency and smooth gameplay while maintaining system stability.
Gaming (No Defender)
For advanced users who fully understand the trade-offs. Removes Windows Defender to maximize available system resources during intensive workloads.
NEXT — Experimental
The future of NuxOS. Early access builds for testing new ideas, configurations, and system changes before they reach stable releases.

Community Project

Built by Enthusiasts, for Enthusiasts

NuxOS is not a commercial product. It is a community project built by enthusiasts who care deeply about operating systems, performance, and long-term reliability.

Every release exists because someone needed it — and decided to build it properly.

NuxOS is built on genuine Microsoft Windows platforms, including Enterprise and Server editions. We do not replace Windows. We refine it — without feature stripping, artificial limitations, or aggressive rebranding.

Who It's For

The Right Tool for the Right Job

Users who want control over their system
Users who understand that optimization involves trade-offs
Users who prefer clarity over hype
Users who value stability as much as performance

If you want one button that "does everything," NuxOS is not for you. If you want the right tool for the right job — welcome.
